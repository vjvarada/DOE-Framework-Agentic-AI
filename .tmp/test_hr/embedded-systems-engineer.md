## About Fracktal Works

Fracktal Works is a pioneering 3D printing and digital manufacturing company founded in 2013. We design and manufacture industrial-grade 3D printers (Julia, Dragon, Twin Dragon, Snowflake, Volterra, Apollo SLS), develop proprietary slicing software (Fracktory), and provide comprehensive 3D printing services across FDM, SLS, HP MJF, SLA, and Vacuum Casting technologies. Our mission is to empower sustainable development through digital fabrication, making high-quality affordable manufacturing accessible to every individual and organization. Based in Bangalore, India, we serve clients across automotive, healthcare, aerospace, manufacturing, jewelry, dental, and entertainment industries.
